​Quantum computing is rapidly evolving from a theoretical concept to a technological reality that will redefine the financial sector. While it brings future opportunities, it also introduces immediate cybersecurity challenges.
​To stay ahead of these challenges, BNPP is actively investing in securing its assets and infrastructure against the emerging quantum threat through a global programme. Work and reflection began as early as 2021, and today, the Group has a dedicated programme to prepare for the migration to quantum-resistant cryptography. Coordinated at the CDF CTP level, this effort involves the Group broadly, with entities and teams (Fortis, CIB, Telecom, DPO, etc.) already heavily engaged in supporting actions and driving progress.
​The purpose of this Bi-Weekly Intelligence Briefing is to demystify this complex topic for all teams. Here is this week’s synthesis regarding the latest advancements in Quantum Computing, their concrete impacts on our industry, and the strategic risks we need to anticipate.
